Electrical Enginnering
A good scientist is a person with original ideas


Department Of Electrical Engineering

“Electrical science has revealed to us the true nature of light added to the exactness of our knowledge.”

Electrical engineering generally deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism, which is further subdivided into a wide range of subfields including electronics, digital computers, power engineering, telecommunications, control systems, instrumentation, and microelectronics. Critical skills needed in electrical engineering include an in-depth understanding of electrical and electronic theory, mathematics and materials. This knowledge allows engineers to design circuits to perform specific functions and meet requirements for safety, reliability and energy efficiency, and to predict how they will behave, before a hardware design is implemented.

This department has got a pool of talented, highly qualified and experienced professors with industrial experience. The department has a strong industry interaction together with commitment to strengthen the students in theoretical and practical aspects. The faculty members of the department are also engaged in active research in coveted institutions.

"Invention is the most important product of man's creative brain. The ultimate purpose is the complete mastery of mind over the material world, the harnessing of human nature to human needs."
